ABOUT ME

Dublin-born and bred. From pulling pints at 16 to leading Microsoft support teams across four countries — every job taught me something. I fix things, I build things, and I lead people. Whether it's a Crestron panel, a ServiceNow workflow, or a team of engineers across five time zones — I figure it out.

A chapter that shaped everything.

Field Sales Agent

Coming ashore and pivoting hard. EMG Sales — Ireland's leading outsourced direct sales operation. Door-to-door, face-to-face, rain or shine across Dublin. Learning to communicate value in 30 seconds and handle rejection gracefully.
